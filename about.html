Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Rana Muhammad Musab Akram, MST (Private) Limited is a SECP-registered software house based in Pakistan, committed to delivering AI-powered, scalable, and secure digital solutions. From developing the PIA Complaints Management System to building custom web and mobile applications, we empower businesses with innovation, agility, and reliability. Our government registration with the Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan (SECP) ensures full compliance and trust in every project we deliver.
